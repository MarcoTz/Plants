Efeutute

Scientific Name Epipremum aureum
Species Type Aracae
Sunlight Requirements indirect
Temperature Range 12.0-30.0
Optimal Temperature Range 18.0-21.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 30cm
pH Range 5.0-6.5
Watering Water once the top layer of soil has dried out
Drain pot if water cannot be fully absorbed
Alternatively, plants can be placed directly in water
Keep in water until the roots have fully soaked
Average days between watering 10
Fertilizing About once every two weeks during growth
Only once every 4-6 weeks in winter
Average days between fertilizing14
Pruning Best pruned in early spring
Remove stems at any length, directly after leaves
Dead leaves should always be removed
Companions Snake Plant
Pothos
Notes Can grow hanging or climbing
For climbing, get some kind of stick it can grow up on
Mist regularly
