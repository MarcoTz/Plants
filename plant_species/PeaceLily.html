Peace Lily

Scientific Name Spathiphullum
Sunlight Requirements indirect
Temperature Range 15.0-40.0
Optimal Temperature Range 16.0-21.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 1cm
pH Range 5.8-6.5
Watering Does not need much water
Water when about 5cm of soil have dried out
Once watering is necessary, leaves will start to droop
Fertilizing Fertilize about once every 6 weeks during growth
Pause during winter and start again before spring
Pruning Remove stem when flowers lose color and droop
Remove yellow and shriveled leaves
When removing a stem, remove directly at the base
Companions Fern
Lemon Bush
Ginger
Notes Direct sunglight will burn leaves
Will not bloom when conditions are too dark
Needs high humidity, mist regularly
Can be grown without any soil, only in water
Keep plant from drafts
Are not actual lily plants
Plants of species Liliana
