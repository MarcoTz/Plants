Common Ivy

Scientific Name Hedera Helix
Sunlight Requirements indirect
Temperature Range -2.0-32.0
Optimal Temperature Range 20.0-30.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 2cm
pH Range 5.5-6.5
Watering Soil should always be moist, but not wet
Check soil to about 5cm down
Water at least once a week
Fertilizing Fertilize about once every 2 weeks
Do not fertilize during stressful periods
Pruning Pruning is not necessary
Long strains can be removed
Companions Climbing Roses
Any other climbing plant
Notes Should sometimes be rinsed off in a sink
The brighter, the better
Any light conditions except full darkness are possible
