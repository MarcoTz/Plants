Tomato

Scientific Name Solanum Lycopersicum
Sunlight Requirements direct
Temperature Range 10.0-40.0
Optimal Temperature Range 12.0-21.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 60cm
pH Range 6.0-7.0
Watering Soil should always be kept moist
Water every few days in the morning
Water need is increased directly after repotting
Fertilizing Fertilize soil before planting
Fertilize every 2 weeks when plants are young
Afterwards only fertilize every 3-4 weeks
Avoid high nitrogen fertilizer
Pruning Remove leaves and stems near the bottom
Remove side shoots before they get big
Companions Basil
Notes Plant outside after last frost
Harden off before moving outside
Use support stalk so they don't fall over
