Chili Pepper

Scientific Name Capsicum anuum
Species Type Capsicum
Sunlight Requirements direct
Temperature Range 5.0-30.0
Optimal Temperature Range 12.0-19.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 70cm
pH Range 6.0-7.5
Watering Soil should be kept moist, but not wet
water around every 2-3 days during growth
Average days between watering 2
Fertilizing Fertilize about every 2 weeks
Dilute fertilizer until plants reach about 30cm in size
Average days between fertilizing14
Pruning Can be pruned at any time, but pruning is not necessary
To prevent bushiness, remove extra side shoots
To prevent legginess, trim main shoot
Keeping a plant smaller can help gaining more fruit
