Pineapple

Scientific Name Ananans Comosus
Sunlight Requirements direct
Temperature Range -2.0-32.0
Optimal Temperature Range 20.0-30.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 50cm
pH Range 4.5-6.5
Watering Water about once a week, when top layer has dried out
Only add little water at a time
Leaves turn yellow when overwatered
Water less during winter
Fertilizing Fertilize every two weeks during growth period
Fertilize only once a month during winter
Always dilute fertilizer
Pruning Regularly remove broken leaves
Cut down after fruit is produced
Companions Herbs
small flowers
Notes Once a fruit has grown, new plants appear next to the base
Replant these to grow new ones
