Optimal Temperature Range 10.0-20.0

Fertilizing Fertilize about once every 6 weeks

Average days between fertilizing42
Pruning Harvested before any pruning is needed
Companions Most vegetables
Notes plant 3-4cm deep
Harvest when leaves turn brown and plants fall over
