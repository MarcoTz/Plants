Ficus

Scientific Name Ficus Benjamina
Sunlight Requirements indirect
Temperature Range 16.0-32.0
Optimal Temperature Range 21.0-26.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 100cm
pH Range 6.0-7.0
Watering Keep soil moist but not wet
Leaves start drooping if overwatered
Fertilizing Fertilize monthly during growth
Outside growth period only fertilize every 2 months
Pruning Prune only after growing period
Best during dormancy in winter
Companions Pothos
Creeping Ficus
Notes New plants can be grown from shoots
Rarely blooms indoors
Mist regularly
Can tolerate direct sunlight, unless there is very strong sunlight
