Polka Dot Plant

Scientific Name Hypoestes Phyllostachy
Sunlight Requirements indirect
Temperature Range 0.0-40.0
Optimal Temperature Range 15.0-26.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 30cm
pH Range 6.0-6.5
Watering Water when top 1-2cm of soil have dried out
Only add little water at a time, better water more often
Fertilizing Fertilize every 2-4 weeks during growth
Reduce fertilizer during winter
Pruning Remove Top leaves and blossoms to promote growth
Cutting blossoms avoids dormancy
Companions Lemon bush
Potato Vine
Notes Mist regularly
Goes dormant after blossoming
New plants can be grown from cuttings
Plants of species Maria
