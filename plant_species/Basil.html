Basil

Scientific Name Ocimum basilicum
Sunlight Requirements direct
Temperature Range 4-35
Optimal Temperature Range 26-32
Minimum Distance to next plant 30cm
pH Range 6-7.5
Watering Soil should always be kept moist
Water at least once a week
Fertilizing Fertilize rarely, at most once a month
When using liquid fertilizer, dilute
Pruning Always remove dead leaves and stems
When removing leaves, always cut right above where new leaves are growing
Companions Tomatoes
Notes
