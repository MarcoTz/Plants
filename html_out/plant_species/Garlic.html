Garlic

Scientific Name Allium Sativum
Sunlight Requirements direct
Temperature Range 0.0-35.0
Optimal Temperature Range 10.0-20.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 15cm
pH Range 5.0-6.3
Watering Water deeply but infrequently
About once a week
Fertilizing Fertilize about once every 6 weeks
Ensure fertilizer contains sulphate
Pruning Harvested before any pruning is needed
Companions Most vegetables
Notes plant 3-4cm deep
Harvest when leaves turn brown and plants fall over
Usually planted in fall before frost, the plants will then start growing in spring
Planting in spring also works, but then there usually won't be any bulbs to harvest
The more sunlight, the better
