Scientific Name Epiprmum aureum
Sunlight Requirements indirect
Temperature Range 12.0-32.0
Optimal Temperature Range 15.0-27.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 30cm
pH Range 6.1-6.5
Watering Check at least once a week, up to 5cm down
Water when checked soil has dried out
Leaves start yellowing when overwatered
Fertilizing Fertilize only once every 2-3 months
Only fertilize between December and May
Pruning Remove dead leaves
Otherwise no pruning necessary
Companions Peace Lily
Money Plant
Notes Mist regularly
When humidity is low, leaves turn brown and crispy
Add sticks it can grow up
