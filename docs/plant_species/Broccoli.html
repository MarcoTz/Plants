Broccoli

Scientific Name Brassica Oleracea
Sunlight Requirements direct
Temperature Range -2.0-35.0
Optimal Temperature Range 18.0-22.0
Minimum Distance to next plant 30cm
pH Range 6.0-7.0
Watering Water about once a week
Water more in hot temperatures
Water thoroughly after repotting
Fertilizing Fertilize about once a month
Fertilize first after transplanting into final pot
Fertilize again after harvesting
Pruning Pruning is generally not necessary
Only remove dead leaves and stems
Companions Celery
Potato
Onion
Herbs
Notes Transplant into final container 4-6 weeks after planting
Roots are usually very shallow
Instead of weeding add mulch
