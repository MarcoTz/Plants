Aloe Vera

Scientific Name Aloe vera
Sunlight Requirements indirect
Temperature Range -4-38
Optimal Temperature Range 13-27
Minimum Distance to next plant 40cm
pH Range 6-6.8
Watering Water deeply, but infrequently
Top third of soil should be dry before watering
Water about every 2-3 weeks
Fertilizing Fertilze once a month during growth
Do not fertilize otherwise
Pruning Always remove damaged or diseased leaves
Remove old outer leaves to promote growth
Companions Herbs
Onion
Notes Leaves can be burnt by bright direct sunlight
Can grow pups next to main plant
Pups can be repotted and grown into new plants

Vera
