Efeutute

Scientific Name Epipremum aureum
Genus Pothos
Family Araceae
Sunlight Requirements Indirect
Temperature Range 12-30
Optimal Temperature Range 18-21
Planting Distance 30
pH Range 5-6.5
Watering Notes Water once the top layer of soil has dried out, Drain pot if water cannot be fully absorbed, Alternatively, plants can be placed directly in water, Keep in water until the roots have fully soaked
Average Watering Days 10
Fertilizing Notes About once every two weeks during growth, Only once every 4-6 weeks in winter
Average Fertilizing Days 14
Pruning Notes Best pruned in early spring, Remove stems at any length, directly after leaves, Dead leaves should always be removed
Companions Snake Plant, Pothos
Notes Can grow hanging or climbing, For climbing, get some kind of stick it can grow up on, Mist regularly
Plants of Species Roselyn, Gabby, Eva Longoria, Gabrielle
